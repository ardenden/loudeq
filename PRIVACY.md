# Privacy Policy — loudeq

loudeq does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It has no network functionality: it never connects to the internet.

The app reads and writes Windows audio device settings (the "Loudness Equalization" enhancement flag) on your own machine only, using the same mechanisms as the Windows Sound control panel. No information leaves your device.

Contact: https://github.com/ardenden/loudeq/issues
